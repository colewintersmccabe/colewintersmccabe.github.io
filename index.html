About Me

Hello my name is Cole Winters-McCabe and I am film and media Major at Franklin & Marshall College. I specialize in 3D graphic design and 3D animation. I got my start with 3D software using autodesk and sketchup in highschool and have since graduated to Blender 3D and cinema 4D. Beyond designing I have also been using the a majority of the adobe suite since my freshmen year of highschool.
